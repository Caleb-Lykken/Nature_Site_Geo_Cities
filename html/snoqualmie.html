Hike in Washington!

In addition to views of the lake itself, hikers can see Roosevelt Peak and Chair Peak, and waterfalls pouring down from these cliffs to meet up with the water below.

Snow Lake is a 7.4 mile roundtrip hike with an elevation gain of 1,800 feet and it is located in Snoqualmie Pass.

Though it may be tempting to hike during winter, hikers need beware of avalanche risk during the steep terrain at the start of the trail when it is snowing.

At the end of the trail, a fork in the road and a choice - one way leading to Middle Fork Snoqualmie Valley, and the other leading to more mountain lake views.

A very popular hike, Snow Lake presents hikers with views of the sparkling lake waters.
